Community Bulletin

The local community council has announced several upcoming development activities to improve public facilities and recreational spaces. Residents are encouraged to participate in awareness programs, cultural events, and environmental campaigns organized throughout the month.

Special workshops on health, education, and technology will also be conducted for students and senior citizens. The city administration aims to strengthen community engagement and improve social interaction among residents through these programs and public initiatives.

Volunteers are invited to join neighborhood cleanliness drives and tree plantation activities scheduled in different zones of the city. Community leaders believe these initiatives will help create a cleaner, safer, and greener environment for everyone.
